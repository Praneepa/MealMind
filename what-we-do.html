Two Simple Steps to Smarter Meals

MealMind AI takes the stress out of meal planning with a simple two-step process powered by artificial intelligence.

How It Works

2 Simple Steps

Step 1

Enter Your Preferences

Start by telling MealMind AI about your household and meal requirements. The more detail you provide, the more accurate your personalised meal plan will be.

Dietary Preferences

Vegetarian, healthy, traditional Sri Lankan, high-protein, or any combination

Cooking Time

Quick meals under 30 minutes, standard, or elaborate weekend cooking

Budget

Set your weekly food budget and AI optimises meals to fit your finances

Serving Size

Single person, couple, or family — portions scale automatically

Ingredients AvailablePro & Family Plans

Tell AI what is already in your fridge and it creates meals from existing groceries

Nutrition GoalsPro & Family Plans

Set calorie targets, macro ratios, and specific nutritional requirements

Step 2

AI Generates Your Personalised Plan

Within seconds, MealMind AI creates a complete weekly meal plan with clear, step-by-step recipes and a smart grocery list tailored to your exact needs. The AI adjusts dynamically each week based on your feedback and changing preferences.

  • Complete 7-day meal plans with breakfast, lunch, and dinner
  • Detailed step-by-step recipes with cooking times
  • Organised grocery list with exact quantities and cost estimates
  • Plans adjust weekly based on your feedback and seasonal ingredients
  • Nutritional breakdown for every meal
