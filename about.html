About Us

Built in Sri Lanka, For Sri Lanka

MealMind AI was born from a simple frustration — the daily stress of deciding what to cook, buying too many groceries, and wasting food and money in the process.

Our Story

From a University Project to a Mission

MealMind AI started as a final-year project at the Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT), affiliated with the University of Westminster. As a computer science student watching my own family struggle with the daily question of "what to cook today," I realised that meal planning was a problem technology could solve — especially for Sri Lankan households.

Every week, my mother would spend hours planning meals, making multiple trips to the Pola, and inevitably throwing away vegetables that went unused. I knew there had to be a better way. That frustration became MealMind AI — an intelligent meal planning platform that generates personalised weekly meal plans with authentic Sri Lankan recipes, organised grocery lists, and budget-friendly options.

What started as a software project quickly grew into a genuine mission: to help Sri Lankan families eat better, spend less, and waste nothing — all through the power of artificial intelligence.

🍳

The Problem

Sri Lankan families waste up to 30% of purchased food due to poor meal planning, overspending at the Pola, and daily cooking indecision.

🤖

The Solution

An AI-powered meal planner that creates personalised weekly plans with Sri Lankan recipes, exact grocery lists, and budget management.

💚

The Impact

Helping 1,200+ families save Rs. 3,000+ weekly, reduce food waste by 40%, and enjoy stress-free, nutritious home-cooked meals.
