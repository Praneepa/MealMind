About Us

Built in Sri Lanka, For Sri Lanka

MealMind AI was born from a simple frustration — the daily stress of deciding what to cook, buying too many groceries, and wasting food and money.

Our Story

From a University Project to a Mission

MealMind AI started as a final-year project at the Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT), affiliated with the University of Westminster. As a computer science student, I noticed that many students living away from home often struggled with the daily question of “what should I cook today?”

Balancing studies, budgets, and busy schedules makes organising daily meals difficult for many students living independently. Deciding what to cook, figuring out what ingredients to buy, and avoiding food waste can quickly become overwhelming. Seeing how common this challenge was among students made me realise that technology could provide a smarter and more practical solution.

That observation led to the creation of MealMind AI — an intelligent meal planning platform that generates personalised weekly plans with practical recipes, organised grocery lists, and budget-friendly options. What started as a software project soon grew into a broader mission: using artificial intelligence to simplify meal planning, reduce food waste, and make everyday cooking easier.

Our Mission

Empowering Families Through Smart Meal Planning

"We believe every Sri Lankan family deserves to eat well without overspending. Our mission is to use AI to eliminate meal planning stress, reduce food waste, and make nutritious home-cooked meals accessible to every household — regardless of budget."

Sri Lankan First

Every recipe, ingredient, and pricing estimate is designed specifically for Sri Lankan households using locally available ingredients.

AI-Driven Innovation

Zero Waste Philosophy
